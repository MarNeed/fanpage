Make sure you know the type of domino game and the specific rules of the the domino game you are playing. You'll soon have a basic idea, after a few rounds of domino play, of which kind of moves or gameplay are the most beneficial. Familiarise yourself with the tiles and type of domino set you are using. Knowing the number of tiles in a particular suit will help you judge the right tile to play and help you work out the possible tiles in other players' hands.
